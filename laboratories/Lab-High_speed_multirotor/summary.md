Lab - Multirotor case study
=======================

Multirotor UAVs are interesting mechatronic systems for sizing, particularly in an educational or academic context. They present a number of dimensioning scenarios involving components from several technologies. Couplings are numerous and strong, particularly in terms of on-board mass. 

Readers interested in this subject may wish to take a closer look at the following references:   

> Budinger, M., Reysset, A., Ochotorena, A., & Delbecq, S. (2020). Scaling laws and similarity models for the preliminary design of multirotor drones. Aerospace Science and Technology, 98, 105658. [Link](https://hal.science/hal-02997598v1)
